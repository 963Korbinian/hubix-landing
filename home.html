The trust layer

Trust isn't a promise.
It's built into every step.

A people-powered network only works if trust is engineered in — not hoped for. So we built it into the mechanics.

Two-way handshake

Every handover is confirmed by both people — giver and receiver. Nothing moves on one person's word alone.

Photo at every step

Each transfer is photographed. A complete visual trail follows the parcel from the first hub to the last hand.

0 lost

Accountable, always

Every parcel is tied to a hub, a connector and a timestamp. If a step is missed, the system knows — and so do you.

Insured

Our story

One man and a brand.
From launch day — all of us.

I came from Germany to Sri Lanka — and stayed. Not for the beaches. For the people.

I saw an island full of energy: shops on every corner, drivers on every road, phones in every hand. Everything was already here — except the network connecting it.

So I started building one. Not for Sri Lanka — with Sri Lanka. Corner shops become hubs. Drivers become connectors. The value stays where the work happens.
