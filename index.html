People move packages for people. Built together. Owned together.

HUBIX uses what Sri Lanka already has — no new trucks, no new warehouses, no big infrastructure. Just two building blocks:

🏪Shops as storage — corner shops, businesses and warehouses become Hubs where packages wait.
🛺People as connectors — anyone who moves through Sri Lanka becomes a Connector who carries packages on their way.

Sending and receiving is the base — every user can send, every user can receive. The Hubs and Connectors make it happen. 🙏

How the network moves

One package. Peer-to-peer.

Every parcel moves from person to person: through Hubs (shops with a bit of storage) and Connectors (people who travel that way anyway). HUBIX just coordinates the flow — you become part of it.

You lose nothing. No upfront costs, no contracts. You only earn when packages move through you.
How do I get paid?+
Directly to your phone via the HUBIX app — every Sunday, automatic. You also choose cash payouts at any Hub.

Yes. Your number stays in Sri Lanka. We never sell it, never share it, never spam you.
