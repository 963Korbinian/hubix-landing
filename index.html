How the network moves

One package. Person to person.

Every package moves through Hubs (shops with a bit of storage) and Connectors.

Just HUBIX connects and coordinates the flow.

And YOU become part of it.

👤 Sender drops off
A customer brings a package to the nearest HUBIX shop — a local shop or storage point.
2
🏠 Hub receives & stores
A Hub is a local shop registered with HUBIX. Fixed opening hours. It's the drop-off and pickup address for the neighborhood.
3
🛺 Connector picks up
A driver gets the job on their phone and moves the parcel to the destination Hub. Nothing gets lost — every handover is a two-way handshake with QR + photo trail. Trust and believe.
4
🏠 Destination Hub
The receiving Hub stores the parcel and notifies the recipient — via WhatsApp if they're not registered yet, or via the HUBIX app + QR code for extra security if they are.
5
👤 Recipient picks up
A quick QR-scan at the Hub. Package delivered. With every shipment, 90% of the money goes to the people doing the work — Connectors and Hubs. Every shipment is a good deed for Sri Lanka.

Quick answers
Does it cost me anything?+
No. Joining is free for Founding Members — and stays free, forever. We take from 10% per delivery, only after you earn.
What if nothing comes of it?+
You lose nothing. No upfront costs, no contracts. You only earn when packages move through you.
How do I get paid?+
Directly to your phone via the HUBIX app — weekly, automatic. Online payout via bank transfer or your favourite cash app. No hub visit needed.
Is my data safe?+
Yes. Your number stays in Sri Lanka. We never sell it, never share it, never spam you.
